# Stately Studio Community

**The Stately Studio will always be free to our Community users** on this free plan, and we will make many future features available on every plan. Request features and check out what we’ve got planned on [our roadmap](https://statelyai.canny.io).

:::studio

We offer a **30-day free trial** on the [Stately Studio Pro account](/studio-pro-plan) so you can explore how our Pro features work for you and your team. You can [upgrade](/upgrade) when you’re signed into the Stately Studio.

:::

## Community features

Our Community plan features include the following:

- Unlimited [public projects](/projects#change-a-projects-visibility)
- [Design and simulate flows](/#studio-editor)
- [Import from code](/import-from-code)
- [Export as code](/export-as-code)
- [XState VSCode extension](/tools/xstate-vscode-extension)
